Modules  
  This course will involve 120 credits of taught modules and a further 60 credits in the form of a dissertation.  
  
  Core modules:  
  
  
   
    Research Skills (20 credits)   
    Practical Translation (20 credits)   
    Bi-lateral Chinese-English Interpreting 1 (20 credits)   
    Bilateral Chines-English Interpreting 2 (20 credits)   
    Translation project/Dissertation (60 credits)   
   
  
  
  Optional modules:  
  
  
   
    Translating Texts  
    Translation Theory   
    Audio-visual Translation
