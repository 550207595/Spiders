Modules  
  The MRes requires 180 credits for completion. The research project is a significant portion of the qualification and comprises 150-160 credits.  You will take a minimum of 20 credits (maximum 30 credits) of taught modules. Typical taught modules can be viewed below:  
  Raw Materials for Brewing  
  Brewery Yeast Management  
  Brewhouse Processes  
  Fermentation and Yeast Handling  
  Beer maturation  
  Brewery Waste Management and Environment Issues  
  Brewing Microbiology  
  Beer flavour development and sensory analysis  
  Packaging of Beer
