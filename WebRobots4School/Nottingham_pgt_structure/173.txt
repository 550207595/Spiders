Modules  
  The MSc offers a range of modules designed specifically to provide fundamental knowledge and practical skills in human factors and ergonomics. Core modules include:  
  
   Cognitive Ergonomics  
   Physical Ergonomics  
   Studying Human Performance  
   Simulation and Digital Human Modelling  
   Risk and Safety Science for Engineers  
   Human-Computer Systems  
   Systems Engineering and Human Factors  
   Advanced Methods in Human Factors  
   Contemporary Issues in Human Factors    
  
  Please note that all module details are subject to change.   
  For more details on our modules, please see the Module Catalogue.
