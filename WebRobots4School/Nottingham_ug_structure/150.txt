Modules  
  The course comprises 120 credits. Many of these credits will be devoted to improving your English language and study skills so that you are able to cope with the demands of undergraduate study. Your other modules will put these skills into practice, focusing on basic principles and methods used in the study of Business and Economics.  
  You will also be helped to improve your communication skills, critical thinking, time management and ability to work independently, which are all essential to successful university study.
